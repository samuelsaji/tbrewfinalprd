Checks during Processing

Throughout the various stages of tea processing, including withering, rolling, drying, and grinding, quality control checks are conducted. These checks involve monitoring factors such as moisture content, color, aroma, and texture.

Taste and Aroma Profiling

Quality assessment includes taste and aroma profiling, which is crucial for determining the flavor characteristics of the tea. Trained tasters or sensory experts evaluate the tea at different stages to ensure that it aligns with the desired taste profile.

Final Quality Assurance

This includes sieving to achieve the desired particle size, visual inspections for any impurities, and sensory evaluations for overall quality. The final product is compared against predetermined standards to confirm that it meets the criteria
